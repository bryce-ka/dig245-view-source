DIG 245 View Source

How to view source

  1. Boot up your computer and launch Google Chrome
  2. Navigate the desired website, if you dont have the web address memorized thats ok, just use google!
  3. Once you're on the site right click the page and click on the inspect option towards the bottom of the list
  4. Voila, now youre in the chrome developer tools.
    Here you can:
    • View/edit the html of the page youre currently on
    • View/edit the css of the page youre currently on
    • View/edit the javascript of the page youre currently on
    • Remove the paywall blocking your article ;)
    • Delete all of the text on the website and view a blank white screen
    • you can even view secret messages from the developer of the page!
  5. I like to call this digging in the weeds of the website
